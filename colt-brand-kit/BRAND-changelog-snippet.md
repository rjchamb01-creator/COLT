> ## Rebrand — June 2026: Talyn → COLT
>
> The product is now **COLT**. "Colts" is the junior grade in rugby league/union — the name reads as
> a young athlete on the rise. Tagline: **"Rise through the grades."**
>
> **Identity (LSKD-inspired: blackout + one bold accent).**
>
> - **Logo:** custom-drawn vector wordmark, tall **condensed grotesque** (curved C/O), font-independent.
>   The **"O" is the only accent**, in Signal Red. Never recolour other letters or add a second accent.
>   App icon = the "C" lettermark (red C on ink, or ink C on red). Assets + component vector live in
>   `Logo.tsx` / `ColtIcon.tsx`.
> - **Palette:** Ink `#0B0B0C` · Bone `#F4F2EC` · **Signal Red `#FF2E1F`** (accent, ~5% of any view —
>   reserve it for the one thing that matters: rank, streak/Heat, primary CTA) · Steel `#6B7280`.
> - **Type:** Saira Condensed (headlines/display) · Archivo (UI + body).
> - **Voice:** unchanged — athlete-first.
> - **Gamification lexicon:** unchanged — Ladder · Caps · Tiers · XP · Squad · Heat.
>
> _Note: only user-facing branding changed. Internal identifiers (package name, Supabase
> tables/columns, env vars, routes) intentionally remain "talyn" to avoid breakage._
