Clear space & minimum size

Keep clear space around the wordmark equal to the width of the “O” on all sides. Minimum legible width: 72 px on screen / 18 mm in print. Below that, switch to the C icon.

The wordmark itself is custom-drawn vector (a tailored condensed grotesque) — not a live font, so it's uniquely yours and renders anywhere.

Do & don't

Do

  • Give it room — respect the clear space.
  • Keep the red for the O only (or one accent moment).
  • Use the C icon when space is tight.
  • Lean into black & bone; let red punch.

Don't

  • Recolour other letters or add a second accent.
  • Stretch, skew, or add effects/shadows.
  • Place on a busy, low-contrast photo.
  • Change the letter spacing or proportions.
